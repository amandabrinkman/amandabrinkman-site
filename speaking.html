Speaking

Keynotes, panels, and fireside conversations on brand strategy, cultural resonance, and narrative that compounds.

Keynotes, panels,
and fireside conversations.

I've presented at SXSW, OMR Hamburg, Duke University, Tulane University, the University of Texas at Austin, University of Pennsylvania, Vanderbilt University, American University, Red Bull, the School of the Art Institute of Chicago, and the Contemporary Arts Center New Orleans, among others.

My talks focus on what it takes to build brands that break through, the mechanics of cultural resonance, and the difference between content that performs once and narrative that compounds. I teach undergraduate courses on pop culture, fan culture, and contemporary media studies at SAIC and the University of New Orleans.
